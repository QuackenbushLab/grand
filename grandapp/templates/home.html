GRAND is a collection of gene regulatory networks of human tissues, cancer, cell lines, and small molecule effects. The networks are bipartite graphs linking Transcription Factors (TFs) and miRNAs to their target genes. GRAND integrates a webserver to test the enrichment of a list of TFs in a set of libraries or find compounds that reverse/exacerbate a gene or TF regulatory profile. To date, we analyzed {{ params.0.genesupin }} gene sets.
GRAND works best with Google Chrome!

Small molecules

173,013 TF and gene targeting scores accounting for 2858 drugs using data from CLUE and DRH.

Cancer

28 cancer-specific TF regulatory networks and 2811 patient-specific networks using data from TCGA and GEO.

Tissues

36 tissue-specific TF and miRNA regulatory networks and 8279 single-sample networks using data from GTEx.

Cell lines

TF and miRNA regulatory networks for 1378 normal and cancer cell lines across 37 tissues of origin using data from CCLE and GTEx.
