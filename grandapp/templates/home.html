    GRAND is a collection of gene regulatory networks of human tissues, cell lines, and small molecule regulatory effects. The networks are represented in bipartite graphs linking Transcription Factors (TFs) to their target genes. Also, you can test the enrichment of a list of TFs in a set of libraries or find compounds that reverse/exacerbate a gene or TF signature. To date, we analyzed {{ params.2.genesupin }} gene sets.
