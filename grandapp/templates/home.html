    GRAND is a collection of gene regulatory networks of human tissues, cancer, cell lines, and small molecule regulatory effects. The networks are bipartite graphs linking Transcription Factors (TFs) to their target genes. Also, you can test the enrichment of a list of TFs in a set of libraries or find compounds that reverse/exacerbate a gene or TF signature. To date, we analyzed {{ params.0.genesupin }} gene sets.

    Small molecules

    2858 drug-induced gene regulatory networks computed from the CLUE gene expression collection and DRH database.

    Cancer

    1638 patient-specific regulatory networks across 4 cancer types using expression data from TCGA and GEO.

    Tissues

    38 tissue-specific and 8279 single-sample gene regulatory network using GTEx gene expression collection.

    Cell lines

    6 cell line gene regulatory networks and many more in development.
